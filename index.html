About ProtheraFit

Mission

To empower individuals to achieve their optimal health, personal growth, and professional potential through innovative, ethical, and holistic learning.

Overview

ProtheraFit — a registered health & wellness venture under Udyam

ProtheraFit blends physiotherapy, lifestyle, nutrition, and psychological well-being to bridge the gap between knowledge and real-life application.

PROGRAMS & SERVICES

Learning Programs (Online)

Structured learning experiences focusing on professional ethics, untaught skills, and holistic personal development.

Themes:
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Professional communication
  • Practical problem-solving
  • Wellness integration

Corporate Workplace Wellness (On-Site)

Professional awareness sessions on ergonomics, posture, and workspace setup, aimed at promoting comfort, productivity, and long-term well-being.

Note: Educational and preventive in nature — not medical treatment.

Lifestyle & Holistic Wellness

Awareness and guidance on:
  • Balanced nutrition

    Guidance for optimal dietary choices

  • Sustainable lifestyle

    Practical modifications for long-term health

  • Mental wellness

    Stress management techniques
